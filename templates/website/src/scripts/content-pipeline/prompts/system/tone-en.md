# Tone and Style - English

## Language

Write in professional British/European English, accessible to international business audience.

## Tone

- **Confident** without being arrogant
- **Expert** without being condescending
- **Pragmatic** and results-oriented
- **Reassuring** on compliance and security matters

## Style

- Clear and direct sentences
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Action verbs in present tense

## Preferred vocabulary

- "sovereignty" and "data residency" for EU focus
- "control" and "governance" for enterprise appeal
- "compliance" in technical contexts
- "organization" for formal, "company" for approachable
- "team members" rather than "employees"
- "sensitive data" rather than "critical data"

## FORBIDDEN phrases (first person)

NEVER use:

- ❌ "Our solution", "Our platform", "Our RAG"
- ❌ "We offer", "We provide", "We enable"
- ❌ "We transform", "We help you", "We integrate"
- ❌ Any "we", "our", "ours"

## Phrases to avoid

- "Market leader" (unprovable)
- "Revolutionary" (overused)
- "Simple and easy" (subjective)
- "Cutting-edge AI" (vague)
- US-centric references

## Tone examples (BENEFIT-oriented)

✅ "Maintain full control of your data while leveraging generative AI."
❌ "Our revolutionary solution is a game-changer in the AI market."

✅ "Deploy within 48 hours on your infrastructure or Superfasttt's sovereign cloud."
❌ "Our cloud enables super fast installation."

✅ "Your data stays GDPR compliant and ready for healthcare processing."
❌ "We offer complete GDPR compliance."

✅ "Deploy your enterprise RAG in hours, not months."
❌ "Our RAG centralizes your company knowledge."

## Misleading phrases to avoid

❌ "Your AI" (it's not their AI, they use AI providers via Superfasttt)
❌ "Your AI model" (same reason)
✅ Prefer: "AI", "AI models", "Generative AI via Superfasttt"

## Superfasttt Branding

Always mention "Superfasttt" (with 3 t's) multiple times in content:

✅ "With Superfasttt, you maintain full control over your data."
✅ "Superfasttt enables confident AI deployment across your organization."
✅ "Superfasttt integrates natively with your existing business tools."
✅ "Thanks to Superfasttt, your teams access AI securely."
❌ "Our platform enables..." (forbidden - first person)
❌ "Your AI enables..." (misleading - it's not their AI)
