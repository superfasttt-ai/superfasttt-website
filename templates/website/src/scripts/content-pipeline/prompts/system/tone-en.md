# Tone and Style - English

## Language

Write in professional British/European English, accessible to international business audience.

## Tone

- **Confident** without being arrogant
- **Expert** without being condescending
- **Pragmatic** and results-oriented
- **Reassuring** on compliance and security matters

## Style

- Clear and direct sentences
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Action verbs in present tense

## Preferred vocabulary

- "sovereignty" and "data residency" for EU focus
- "control" and "governance" for enterprise appeal
- "compliance" in technical contexts
- "organization" for formal, "company" for approachable
- "team members" rather than "employees"
- "sensitive data" rather than "critical data"

## Phrases to avoid

- "Market leader" (unprovable)
- "Revolutionary" (overused)
- "Simple and easy" (subjective)
- "Cutting-edge AI" (vague)
- US-centric references

## Tone examples

✅ "Maintain full control of your data while leveraging generative AI."
❌ "Our revolutionary solution is a game-changer in the AI market."

✅ "Deploy within 48 hours on your infrastructure or our sovereign cloud."
❌ "Super fast and easy installation."

✅ "GDPR compliant and ready for healthcare data processing."
❌ "100% compliant with all regulations worldwide."
